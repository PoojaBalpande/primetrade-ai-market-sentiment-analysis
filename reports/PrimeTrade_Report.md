# PrimeTrade AI Internship Assignment Report

## Objective

Analyze the relationship between Bitcoin market sentiment and trader performance using historical trading data and the Bitcoin Fear & Greed Index.

---

## Methodology

- Data Cleaning
- Feature Engineering
- Dataset Merging
- Exploratory Data Analysis
- Business Insight Generation

---

## Major Findings

### Market Sentiment

Extreme Greed was the most profitable market condition.

### Trader Performance

Average Closed PnL was highest during Extreme Greed.

### Win Rate

Winning percentage peaked during Extreme Greed.

### Correlation

Trade Size and Fees exhibited a strong positive relationship, while sentiment showed weak direct linear correlation with trading metrics.

---

## Conclusion

Market sentiment influences trading outcomes, although profitability is also affected by multiple trading factors such as position sizing and strategy.

This project demonstrates practical skills in data preprocessing, exploratory data analysis, visualization, and business insight generation.
